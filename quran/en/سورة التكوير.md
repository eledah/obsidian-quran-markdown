# سورة التكوير
#Meccan
▶ [[سورة عبس]] | [[سورة الانفطار]] ◀
##### 1
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the sun (with its spacious light) is folded up;">بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ إِذَا ٱلشَّمْسُ كُوِّرَتْ</span>
##### 2
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the stars fall, losing their lustre;">وَإِذَا ٱلنُّجُومُ ٱنكَدَرَتْ</span>
##### 3
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the mountains vanish (like a mirage);">وَإِذَا ٱلْجِبَالُ سُيِّرَتْ</span>
##### 4
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the she-camels, ten months with young, are left untended;">وَإِذَا ٱلْعِشَارُ عُطِّلَتْ</span>
##### 5
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the wild beasts are herded together (in the human habitations);">وَإِذَا ٱلْوُحُوشُ حُشِرَتْ</span>
##### 6
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the oceans boil over with a swell;">وَإِذَا ٱلْبِحَارُ سُجِّرَتْ</span>
##### 7
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the souls are sorted out, (being joined, like with like);">وَإِذَا ٱلنُّفُوسُ زُوِّجَتْ</span>
##### 8
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the female (infant), buried alive, is questioned -">وَإِذَا ٱلْمَوْءُۥدَةُ سُئِلَتْ</span>
##### 9
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="For what crime she was killed;">بِأَىِّ ذَنۢبٍۢ قُتِلَتْ</span>
##### 10
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the scrolls are laid open;">وَإِذَا ٱلصُّحُفُ نُشِرَتْ</span>
##### 11
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the world on High is unveiled;">وَإِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ كُشِطَتْ</span>
##### 12
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When the Blazing Fire is kindled to fierce heat;">وَإِذَا ٱلْجَحِيمُ سُعِّرَتْ</span>
##### 13
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="And when the Garden is brought near;-">وَإِذَا ٱلْجَنَّةُ أُزْلِفَتْ</span>
##### 14
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="(Then) shall each soul know what it has put forward.">عَلِمَتْ نَفْسٌۭ مَّآ أَحْضَرَتْ</span>
##### 15
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="So verily I call to witness the planets - that recede,">فَلَآ أُقْسِمُ بِٱلْخُنَّسِ</span>
##### 16
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="Go straight, or hide;">ٱلْجَوَارِ ٱلْكُنَّسِ</span>
##### 17
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="And the Night as it dissipates;">وَٱلَّيْلِ إِذَا عَسْعَسَ</span>
##### 18
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="And the Dawn as it breathes away the darkness;-">وَٱلصُّبْحِ إِذَا تَنَفَّسَ</span>
##### 19
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="Verily this is the word of a most honourable Messenger,">إِنَّهُۥ لَقَوْلُ رَسُولٍۢ كَرِيمٍۢ</span>
##### 20
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="Endued with Power, with rank before the Lord of the Throne,">ذِى قُوَّةٍ عِندَ ذِى ٱلْعَرْشِ مَكِينٍۢ</span>
##### 21
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="With authority there, (and) faithful to his trust.">مُّطَاعٍۢ ثَمَّ أَمِينٍۢ</span>
##### 22
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="And (O people!) your companion is not one possessed;">وَمَا صَاحِبُكُم بِمَجْنُونٍۢ</span>
##### 23
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="And without doubt he saw him in the clear horizon.">وَلَقَدْ رَءَاهُ بِٱلْأُفُقِ ٱلْمُبِينِ</span>
##### 24
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="Neither doth he withhold grudgingly a knowledge of the Unseen.">وَمَا هُوَ عَلَى ٱلْغَيْبِ بِضَنِينٍۢ</span>
##### 25
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="Nor is it the word of an evil spirit accursed.">وَمَا هُوَ بِقَوْلِ شَيْطَٰنٍۢ رَّجِيمٍۢ</span>
##### 26
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="When whither go ye?">فَأَيْنَ تَذْهَبُونَ</span>
##### 27
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="Verily this is no less than a Message to (all) the Worlds:">إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكْرٌۭ لِّلْعَٰلَمِينَ</span>
##### 28
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="(With profit) to whoever among you wills to go straight:">لِمَن شَآءَ مِنكُمْ أَن يَسْتَقِيمَ</span>
##### 29
<span class="ayah hovertext" data-hover="But ye shall not will except as Allah wills,- the Cherisher of the Worlds.">وَمَا تَشَآءُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ رَبُّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ</span>
